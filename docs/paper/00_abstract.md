# The Predictive Universe

Results labeled Theorem/Proposition/Lemma are internal consequences of the stated formal branch and hypotheses in which they appear. PPI is not a discretionary copy map from mathematics into physics: physical content is represented by finite protocol-response presheaves, response-null labels are quotiented, and PCE selects the least-cost representative in the admissible finite-response class.
